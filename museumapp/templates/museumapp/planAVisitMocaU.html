Where, When and How
Welcome to MoCA. A visit to the museum is the best way to reconnect with 500 years of Goa’s unique art and culture. With a collection unlike any in the world, a visit to the Museum of Christian Art is the best way to start your journey through Old Goa.
How to book your ticket?
Museum's admission fee is Rs 100 for adults and Rs 50 for students. For more details, visit our Book Ticket or Guided Tour page. Book your ticket in advance to make sure you don't miss out!
